Main Menu

Instructions:
Use the Buttons located at the top of each screen to navigate through this Website. If you ever forget about any of the Instructions, use the "Main Menu" button, to get back to this Page.

To see, how many days you used this Application, click on the "Calendar" button. There you will see a Calender with marked entries, for the days you finished at least one exercise. You may also download your current progress by clicking the "export" button on the "Calendar" Page.

The look and sound of the Website may be customized on the "Options" page. To get there, click the "Options" button to the right of the "Calendar" button. On this page you will see two container. On the left will be a selection of available songs, that may be played on this website. To try out a single song, just click it. It should start to play imediately and will stop to play by switching the page, reloading the page or if the song ends. You may also create a song playlist, which will continously play on this website. To do so, drag a song from the left container to the right. The right container is labeled "Song Playlist". You can always remove a song from a playlist. If you created a Playlist you are happy with, save it, by clicking the "save and play" button located at the top of the right container.
Furthermore, you can select a background image for this website. Click "Select file" on the bottom of the "Options" page and select the image you want to use.

The button "Main Menu" will bring you back to this currently displayed page. Don't hesitate to click it, if you forgot about a feature that you want to use.

The "Exercises" page can be reached by a single click on the "Exercises" button. On this page all the available Exercises will be displayed. You can see the title, the duration, the areas you will train and a step by step guide. To start a single Exercise, click the "Start" button, located on the bottom right of a Exercise. To start multiple Exercises at once, you have to create a "Exercise playlist". To do so, enter a name for the playlist in the textbox on the left of the "Start" button and hit the "Add to playlist" button just in between the "Start" button and the textbox. Beware that each playlist will only hold a single copy of each added Exercise. To upload a self made exercise, please read click the "Upload Exercise" button located just atop the Exercises. For instructions on how to create a Exercise, open the "ExerciseSyntax.md" file.

All your created Exercise Playlists will be displayed on the "Exercise Playlists" page, which can be reached by clicking the "Exercise Playlists" button on the very right. If you never created a single Exercise playlist you will be notified that you can create Exercise playlists on the "Exercises" page. Each Exercise playlist will display the Exercises that you added to the Exercise playlist. Here you can start a single Exercise similiar to starting a Exercise on the "Exercises" page or you can start a whole Exercise playlist by clicking the "Start Exercise Playlist" located on the top of each exercise playlist.

After starting a exercise or exercise playlist, you are directed to the "Exercise" page. On the left you will see the image that your camera captured. On this image you should see green dots all over your body, which will show you, where the application thinks your body is. If there are no dots displayed, please reload the page. To start exercising, press "Load next Exercise" on the top right. The instructions for the "Grundhaltung" of the first exercise will now be displayed. After you feel ready to start the Exercise, click "Start Exercise". A timer will count down and a new instruction will be displayed. It will tell you which pose is the next pose. After the timer reached zero, the application will evaluated your posture. After completing a Exercise, the timer will disappear and the "Load next Exercise" button will be displayed again. If you completed all exercises, the website will notify you about your Score and the "Exit" button will be displayed on the top right. This button will bring you back to the page that you came from. If this was your first exercise of the day, the calender will be updated.
